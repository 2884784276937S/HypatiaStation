26th May 2013

2884784276937S updated:

  • Admin reports. No admins online? Bad admins online? Report it, for other admins/mods to review. Detailed information and logs are recorded when a report is created, to ensure exactly what happened is determined.
  • Cloning. We now have a brand new cloning system? Not scared of death? You will be now. Cloning now requires incredibly expensive stem-cells, which of course you can break, and then... once you've gone through the excruciating cloning itself; you'll have to undergo regenerative medical care, and of course build up the strength to walk and pick up things.
  • Cloning sprites have been added; replacing the amusing "FUK DIS SHIT NUMBERS" sprites that somehow snuck through.
  • AIs can now send CentCom reports. And don't worry, unlike with the comms console (which you can still use for this) when used by personnel, you're not going to be BSAd fo rthe sheer cost o fthe message.
  • Lockdowns, AIs are now able to selectively lock down certain areas or the entire station. Lockdowns include blast doors, firedoors, and airlocks.
  • Breach detection. Worried about playing an AI and not being able to keep up? Well now as AI, if a breach or sudden degradation in atmospherics occurs, you'll be notified with brief information and a hyperlink to jump to.
  • Previously non-functional spam filters are now in place, working.
  • Adding some realism and futurism to cyborg, soon you will be able to scan things with varying coloured search lights, among other things.
  • Cyborgs are currently recieving some additional modules and potential upgrades, making cyborg's jobs a little bit easier.
  • As much as you all might hate it, admins and moderators are now able to forcefully open a special rule window. That's right, they can selectively open a rule windows ot either players, players + donors, or everyone! They'll also be able to set a time period for which it can't be minimized or closed (don't worry, the limit is 60 seconds, but even that should never happen).
  • Some general bugfixes I've likely forgot of.
