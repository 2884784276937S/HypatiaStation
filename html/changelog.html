TwistedAkai

30th April 2013 updated:

  • AIs, Cyborgs, and pAIs now have station time in their status panel.
  • Attempting to access Character Setup after declaring ready now sets you to not ready, unlocking your setup. This fixes an existing bug that could cause your setup to lock by declaring ready after the round has started.

25th April 2013

TwistedAkai updated:

  • Removed Jungle away mission from rotation.

24th April 2013

2884784276937S updated:

  • (ADMINS) Ability to admin-change security level
  • Constructable airlock cycling systems
  • Now able to order pressure tanks from cargo, as well as able to order canisters from cargo.
  • Pressure tanks can now be wrenched/un-wrenched and subsequently moved around.

23rd April 2013

2884784276937S updated:

  • pAIs now feature a crew manifest much like PDAs, including SSD status.
  • Newcasters are now fixable.
  • (ADMINS) Admins now have instant-respawn, "GO ADMIN POWERS! GO!"
  • Changes to chemistry, added a new machine and an additional chemical dispenser.
  • Tweaks to the Donor rank.
  • (ADMINS) The "Dsay" proc is now accessible to all admins and moderators (and donors), will display appropriately. E.g. if moderator, "MOD(Annedu)", if donor "DONOR(2884784276937S)", if coder, "DEV(2884784276937S)", etc.
  • (ADMINS) Banhammer button. Tired of not being able to play that banhammer sound, lack of permissions? Well now you can, with the all new - BANHAMMER!
  • pAI door jack now unbolts doors, instead of just opening them.
  • (ADMINS) Moderators can now change their OOC colour.
  • Various additional spam filters, namely preventing white space spam - and falsifying server messages. As done several days ago.
  • Xenoarcheology's cyclcing doors, should, be fixed.
  • Revamping pAIs. Currently barely anything noticeable has been done.
  • Various additional bug-fixes, I've forgotten half of them.

21st April 2013

2884784276937S updated:

  • Added "Chemical Preparation Crate"
  • Cyborgs can now open and close crates as well as lockers. Previously they could only open.
  • Newcasters can now be constructed. Circuits can be made at research.
  • Newscasters now accept the BBCode used on paper. Some changes may be made at a later point.
  • Various maximum input lengths have been extended. We trust you won't abuse this.
  • We now have a changelog!
